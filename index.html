Utah

Utah is located within the western United States. It is in the middle of Colorado, Wyoming, Idaho, Arizona, and Nevada. It also shares a corner with New Mexico. Utah is known for the skiing slopes that are located in the mountains. It is also known for the unique rock formations such as the Arches. It is also where The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints is headquartered.
